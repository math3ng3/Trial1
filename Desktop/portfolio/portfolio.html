About me;

Andrew Mathenge born and raised in Nairobi, Kenya. I moved to the city as an infant with my parents, who later enrolled me into Catholic parochial school, where I sat my KCPE National exam and excelled without a doubt. Upon my completion of primary school I joined Upper Hill School in the year 2016 and completed in 2019, where again I did fairly well. I then joined my friends, who were running a sticker shop known as Kl0ut stickerz and there I gained salemanship know how. Unfortunately the pandemic struck and we were forced to close down our shop and sadly had to part ways.
So in my free time, I strated checking out easy to relate to, computer courses with my highschool based knowledge. That's where I stumbled into Software Development course at Moringa School. As I dug deeper and deeper I got fascinated by the course but the facts that captivated me most were; One, that software Devlopment doesnt have a constant learning curve and secondly, the fact that it actually encourages creativity.
